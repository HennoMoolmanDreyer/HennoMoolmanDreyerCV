I grew up on a farm near the small town of Bothaville in the Free State. It was an interest that turned into a passion for technology that convinced me to study B.Sc. Information Technology.

I am technically versatile; a quick learner and have the ability to adapt exceptionally well to new environments, given my culturally diverse background. What else would you expect with my german mother, afrikaans father, farmboy upbringing, student life in Potchefstroom and a South African and German passport ? I am fluent in 12 languages: Afrikaans, German, English, C++, Java, Python and many more.

Life has taught me to have a tough skin and I believe that is a good quality to have in my line of study. My best characteristics are my curiosity to always learn more and my stubbornness to never give up.

I trust that you would like to get to know me better, if so, head to my contact page and say hello.
